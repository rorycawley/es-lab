# The Companies Registration Act

*Fictional legal instrument. Created for use with the es-lab micro companies registry.*

---

## Part 1 — Registration

**Section 1 — Eligibility**
A private limited company may be registered by one or more natural persons.

**Section 2 — Application Requirements**
An application to register a company must state:
- A proposed company name
- At least two proposed directors
- A registered office address

**Section 3 — Proposed Directors**
Every proposed director named in an application must be a natural person whose identity has been verified by the Registry.

**Section 4 — Examination**
The Registry shall examine every application for compliance with this Act, including whether the registered office address is plausible, valid, and usable as a registered office.

**Section 5 — Approval**
The Registrar shall approve an application if it complies with this Act. Upon approval, the company is registered and enters the Register.

**Section 6 — Rejection**
The Registrar shall reject an application that does not comply with this Act, stating the reason.

**Section 7 — Withdrawal**
An applicant may withdraw an application at any time before a decision is made.

## Part 2 — The Register

**Section 8 — The Register**
The Registrar shall maintain a register of registered companies. The register is the authoritative legal record.
